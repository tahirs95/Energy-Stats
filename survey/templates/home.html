Welcome to NYC Key Energy Compliance

Check to see if your building is in compliance with the "Climate Mobilization Act" - New York City Local Law 97 of 2019
https://www1.nyc.gov/assets/buildings/local_laws/ll97of2019.pdf

This law requires buildings in NYC that are larger than 25,000 square feet and do not have special protection to comply with energy efficiency standards. The standards limit the amount of Electricity, Natural Gas, Fuel Oil and Steam a building can use. The "ACT" converts the use of Electricity, Natural Gas, Fuel Oil and Steam into CO2.

NOT COMPLYING WITH THE "ACT" CAN BE VERY EXPENSIVE

If the building doesn't file a compliance report it will be fined $0.50 per square foot per month the report isn't filed.
If a building uses too much CO2 it will fined $268 for every unit the building goes over the limit.
If a building files a report with false material statements it can be fined $500,000 and even jailed up to 30 days.
This site is designed to provide your building a report that details how your building can comply with the "ACT". This report is FREE. You will receive it INSTANTLY once you've entered your building's information. Creating a profile is optional. By creating a profile your building information will be saved, you can add or change information to your profile and receive updated reports.
