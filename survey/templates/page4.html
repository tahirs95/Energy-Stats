Determining Building Usage Type

BUILDING GROUP

Your building's compliance requirements are based on the usage type or group. Building groups include A, B, B*, E, F, H, I-1, I-2, I-3, I-4, M, R-1, R-2, S, U. If you know your building group you can choose from the options below. To find out which group your building belongs to continue below.
Follow the key to figure out which group your building belong to:

KEY

A => Gathering (75 or more persons) civic and social gatherings, religious, recreation, food or drink, awaiting transportaion

B => Office, professional, service

B* => Civic administrative facility for emergency response, non-production laboratory, ambulatory health care facility

E => Through 12th grade if over 12th grade see Assembly (A)

F => Factory (non-high hazard)

H => Industrial (high hazard)

I-1 => Care Facilities (adult)

I-2 => Medical, surgical, psychaitric, nursing

I-3 => Restraint or Security

I-4 => Custodial care facilities (children)

M => Retail, Markets

R-1 => Housing less than 1 month

R-2 => Permanent housing

S => Warehouse, Freight depot, Distribution center

U => not defined above (rare)



For detailed information on building group visit: https://up.codes/viewer/new_york_city/nyc-building-code-2014/chapter/3/use-and-occupancy-classification#3
